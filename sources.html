Method note

How I treated correlations.

The site uses a five-election assembly series. That is enough to form directional campaign assumptions, not enough to claim statistical causation. Where I cite correlations, I use them as strategist signals: turnout and electorate growth move with BJP share; LDF and UDF share move inversely with BJP share; candidate effects explain the jumps better than any single structural variable.
